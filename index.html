Snacks, Dessert & Overnight Oats

Daily snack stack, evening ritual, and the full trail mix recipe.

🥜 Weekly Trail Mix — Ratio Recipe
Make one batch Sunday (~10.5 oz / 7 servings). Store in a jar. Grab a small bag each morning.
2 parts shelled pistachios (roasted, unsalted)
1 part almonds or cashews
1 part pumpkin seeds (pepitas)
1 part dried cherries or cranberries (unsweetened)
½ part dark chocolate chips (70%+ cacao)
½ part unsweetened coconut flakes, toasted
Serving: 1.5 oz / 42g (~¼ cup heaping) · ~200 cal · 6–7g protein · 14g fat · 13g carbs
Weekly batch: Use 2 cups pistachios as your base unit — scale everything proportionally. Yields ~10.5 oz / 7 servings.
Tip: Toast pumpkin seeds and coconut flakes in a dry pan 3–4 min first. Makes a big difference in flavor.
🥣 Daily Snack & Dessert Stack
~28g protein from snacks + evening. Anchored by edamame.
Edamame (daily anchor)
1 cup frozen steam-in-bag. Microwave fresh each day — no prep. ~17g protein, ~180 cal. Salt lightly.
Trail Mix (daily)
1.5 oz / 42g portion. ~7g protein, ~200 cal. Pre-portion into bags Sunday morning.
String Cheese (backup)
1–2 sticks on high-training days. ~7g protein each. Convenient at your desk.
Evening Dessert (daily)
¼c whole-grain granola + ¼c soy milk. ~150 cal, ~3–4g protein. Built into daily calorie target. Small, satisfying, consistent.
Overnight Oats Base
1c soy milk + ½c rolled oats + 1 tbsp chia seeds + 1 tsp honey. ~10g protein, ~320 cal. Add fresh toppings each morning.

Sauces, Dressings & Prep Recipes

One double-batch sauce per week: half for grain bowl dressing, half as protein marinade.

🫙 Double-Batch System

Make ~1 cup of the week's sauce on Sunday. Half goes in a jar for bowl drizzling all week. Half marinates the sheet pan protein before roasting. One recipe, two uses, zero extra effort.

Sunday Base Prep

Same every week regardless of cuisine rotation.
