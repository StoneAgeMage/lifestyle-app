Chicken Lentil Soup

Recipe Book: Rowing Meal Plan Book
Servings: 6 servings
Active Time: 10 minutes
Total Time: 45 minutes

Ingredients

- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 yellow onion (diced)
- 3 carrots (diced)
- 2 celery ribs (diced)
- 4 garlic cloves (chopped)
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1/2 tsp cumin
- 1 ½ cups green lentils (rinsed)
- 1 ½ lb. chicken breast (cut into bite-sized pieces)
- 14 oz. fire roasted diced tomatoes (with their juices)
- 6 cups chicken broth
- 5 oz. spinach
- salt and pepper to taste

Directions

1. Heat olive oil in a dutch oven or heavy bottomed pot over medium heat, then add the onions, carrots and celery and sauté for 6 minutes, or until soft. Then, add the garlic, salt, smoked paprika, and cumin and sauté for 1 minute.

2. Pour in the lentils, along with the diced chicken, diced tomatoes, and broth. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce heat to a simmer and simmer until the lentils are cooked, about 25 minutes.

3. Last, stir in the spinach until wilted, then add salt and pepper to taste. Enjoy!
