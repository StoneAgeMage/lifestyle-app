Korean Braised Tofu (Dubu Jorim)

Recipe Book: Rowing Meal Plan Book
Servings: 4 servings
Active Time: 5 minutes
Total Time: 20 minutes

Ingredients

- 1 package of firm tofu (package size is 14oz)
- 1/4 cup light soy sauce
- 1/4 cup water
- 1 tbsp gochugaru
- 1/2 tbsp maple syrup
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1/2 tbsp sesame seeds
- 2 cloves garlic (minced)
- 1 scallion (finely chopped)
- 2 tbsp avocado oil
- 1 cup dry brown rice
- 2 heads of broccoli, trimmed and steamed

Directions

1. Drain the tofu from the package and pat dry with a paper towel. Cut the tofu lengthwise into 1/2" thick pieces.

2. In a small bowl, mix soy sauce, water, gochugaru, sugar, sesame oil, sesame seed, garlic, and scallions to form the sauce.

3. In a medium pan, heat 2 tbsp of neutral oil over medium high heat. Pan fry the tofu pieces for 2-3 minutes until golden brown, flip and continue cooking for 2-3 minutes until the other side is golden brown.

4. Add the sauce and braise the tofu for 5-6 minutes over medium heat until the sauce has reduced and thickened slightly. Serve with steamed rice and enjoy!
