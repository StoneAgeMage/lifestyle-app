Overnight Oats Recipes

Recipe Book: Rowing Meal Plan Book
Servings: 1 serving
Active Time: 5 minutes
Total Time: 4 hours 5 minutes

Ingredients

- ½ c rolled oats
- 1 T chia seeds
- 1 T ground flax
- 1 c unsweetened unflavored soy milk
- ½ tsp cinnamon
- 2 tsp maple syrup
- ¼ c blueberries
- 1/4 c sliced apple

Directions

1. Combine base ingredients plus all ingredients for desired flavor in a bowl or jar that can be sealed and stir well.

2. Refrigerate overnight.

3. Add an extra splash of milk if needed before serving. Serve hot or cold.


Notes

Typically meal prep this in batches of 4-5 for the week
